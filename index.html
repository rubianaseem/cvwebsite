I'm passionate about digital marketing. I help in planning a perfect business strategy, marketing budget, and implement using the latest technology and tools. Successful in project management on various niche. Have handled over 50+ projects in the past 9 years.

Howdy!

I like to introduce myself as Rubia Naseem. I started my digital marketing journey in early 2012 when I was pursuing my college studies. Initially, I had a great passion for writing. One of the clients looked at my personal blog and gave me a project for writing his website content.

Later in 2014, I started learning other modules of digital marketing which involve Social Media Marketing, Email Marketing, SEO and Search Engine Marketing.

Additionally, I took a advanced digital marketing certification course after which I got a few interesting opportunities on managing a team of 10-15 members as a digital marketing manager. The role was challenging and yet the experience that I gained was valuable.

My years of experience in Digital Marketing has then helped me to bag Digital project manager role for a client based in Canada. It was a something new for me and I had a great time handling a team of over 20 people.

Finally before pursuing my higher studies dream in UK, I got a great opportunity to work with a UK Client for optimizing website, plan a strategy for Facebook ads, set and monitor the campaign. I was able to give good results for the campaigns on Facebook.
